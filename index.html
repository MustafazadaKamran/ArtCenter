The Sanat Art Centre, which recently opened its doors, has become a citadel of inspiration and creativity.

The center offers creative activities for children and teenagers aged from 4 to 18. The center offers creative classes in sculpture, painting and drawing, music and vocals, dance and acting, and chess.
